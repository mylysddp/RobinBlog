## Why routine deletion matters
- **Protect context:** Old posts can age poorly or be misread. Deleting stale tweets reduces misunderstandings.
- **Stay on-brand:** A focused timeline helps followers understand what you stand for today.
- **Limit data trails:** Removing old engagement trims the personal data you broadcast.
- **Improve searchability:** When clutter is gone, the tweets that matter are easier to find.

## Tips for a healthy Twitter routine
- Use a 90-day filter once a month to keep your recent voice front-and-center.
- Clear high-engagement but outdated promotions to avoid confusing new followers.
